Alkali
In my second year of university my class and I were briefed to create channel branding for an imaginary channel. I chose to create a multi-disciplinary arts channel and came up with this solution. I wanted to explore how to include anything that would be broadcast on this channel so chose to get quite experimental with it. With this I decided to take materials to their most basic forms and went with this wireframe look.
