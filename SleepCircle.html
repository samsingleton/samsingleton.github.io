SleepCircle
This motion piece is for my third year negotiated brief. We were asked to answer either a live or competition brief. I chose to answer the RSA Sleep Matters brief partnered with Samsung. The piece is an explainer video for a product/service that allows a user to easily find information about sleep, without the conflicts that can be seen online. The elevator pitch for the idea is "SleepCircle is an interactive easily accessible tool used to present information about getting into the rhythm of good sleep." I believe it has the ability to empower someone to easily alter their lifestyle to their needs with no nagging. You choose to do what you’d like!
