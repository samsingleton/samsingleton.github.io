Think Before You Share.
Our data is becoming more and more at risk, the general public doesn’t know where their data is going or what it’s used for. This motion piece explore the potential future of our data, and highlights the risks of oversharing online. This piece was created for my typography unit during my third year of motion graphics. I wanted to explore false UI design which I found very exciting during the creation of the piece.
